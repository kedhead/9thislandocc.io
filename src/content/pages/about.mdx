## Welcome to 9th Island OCC

9th Island Outrigger Canoe Club brings the spirit of aloha and Hawaiian outrigger canoe paddling tradition to Las Vegas. We are a community of paddlers dedicated to the sport of outrigger canoe racing and the cultural traditions that come with it.

### Our Mission

We strive to:

* Promote the sport of outrigger canoe paddling
* Build a strong ohana (family) of paddlers
* Share Hawaiian culture and traditions
* Compete in regional and national races
* Welcome paddlers of all skill levels

### What We Do

* **Practice Sessions**: Regular training on Lake Mead
* **Racing**: Compete in regional and national outrigger canoe races
* **Community Events**: Beach cleanups, cultural celebrations, and social gatherings
* **Beginner Programs**: Introduction to outrigger paddling for new members

### Join Us

Whether you're an experienced paddler or completely new to the sport, we welcome you to our ohana. Contact us to learn more about joining 9th Island OCC.
